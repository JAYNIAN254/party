Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to the party?
Dress to impress! Most of our parties have themes, so check the event details for specific dress code information. Comfortable shoes are recommended for dancing.
What's included in the ticket price?
Entry to the event, access to all areas, and sometimes complimentary drinks or food depending on the event. VIP tickets include additional perks like reserved seating and premium drinks.
Is there parking available?
Limited parking is available at most venues. We recommend using ride-sharing services or taxis for convenience and safety.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend?
Tickets are non-refundable but transferable. You can transfer your ticket to someone else by contacting us at least 24 hours before the event.
